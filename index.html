What is IMS+?

A secure platform built to connect healthcare and medical tourism with the aid of artificial intelligence, distributed and decentralized technologies.

Introduction

Medical Tourism is the concept of people travelling between countries in order to get highly quality medical services for a lesser price. Sri Lanka is a country widely known as a popular tourist destination as well as a quality medical services provider. These facts has created a niche market in Sri Lanka for Meical Tourism.

The healthcare industry constantly deals with sensitive data. Most healthcare service providers in Sri Lanka are already adapting to digitalized systems. It is crucial that these systems provide secure methods of transferring and storing data.

IMS+ provides a platform that connects patients both foreign and local with doctors and secure storage and transfer methods for patient data. IMS+ also features services such as patient treatment prediction, voice-to-text services, virtual clinic room etc.
