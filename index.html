What is IMS+?

A secure platform built to connect healthcare and medical tourism with the aid of artificial intelligence, distributed and decentralized technologies.

Introduction

Medical Tourism is the concept of people travelling between countries in order to get highly quality medical services for a lesser price. Sri Lanka is a country widely known as a popular tourist destination as well as a quality medical services provider. These facts has created a niche market in Sri Lanka for Meical Tourism.

The healthcare industry constantly deals with sensitive data. Most healthcare service providers in Sri Lanka are already adapting to digitalized systems. It is crucial that these systems provide secure methods of transferring and storing data.

IMS+ provides a platform that connects patients both foreign and local with doctors and secure storage and transfer methods for patient data. IMS+ also features services such as patient treatment prediction, voice-to-text services, virtual clinic room etc.

Research Problem

Automated Diagnosis

Inability to perform a diagnosis without the presence of a doctor.


Data Security

Existing methods of storage and sharing mechanisms are lacking in integrity, compatibility and portability.

Outdated technologies

Current methods in use for writing prescriptions has proven to be inefficient.

Unclear Documents

Most prescriptions issued by doctors are illegible due to handwriting.

Research Objectives

Implementing disease identification system
The systemis to be designed to take symptoms as inputs, analyze symptoms using AI technology and provide the identified disease as the output.
Implementing a map with details of doctors and hospitals
The system is to be designed to show details of doctors and hospitals where they work in a map after identifying the disease.
Implementing an EHR system with AI assistant
The system is to be designed to analyze medical reports of the patient using AI assistant and generate EHR using customizable templates. The system is able to generate charts according to details of EHR using AI assistant and the system is to be designed as a voice controllable system.
Implementing blockchain based EHR access controlling system
The system is to be designed as a patient focusing EHR access controlling system to provide access to view all the details by patient and provide access to healthcare providers to view only allowed details by patient. It is allowed to add new details to EHR by healthcare provider, but no one have access to edit existing details.
Implementing blockchain based medical reports sharing system
The system is to be designed as the patient is able to send medical reports likewise blood reports, x-ray reports and receive prescription reports from the healthcare provider.
Implementing document prediction system
By identifying trends and patterns in medical documents taken for a diagnosis, the system suggests the medical documents that will most likely be requested by the doctor.
Implementing virtual clinic room
The system is to be designed to connect patient and doctor virtually and it will provide the list of available doctors to select according to the requirements of the patient.

Methodology

IMS+ is a distributed system developed with blockchain technology. It allows the system to securely transfer and store sensitive data. The blockchain ledger allows to keep track of user activity through the system. Each hospital acts as a node in the blockchain network.

With the aid of artificial intelligence, the system is capable of predicting the treatment once the symptoms are provided by the patient. Also the system is capable of predicting the document that the doctor might requestat the time of diagnosis.

The system also has a voice-to-text model which aids the doctor to create electronic health records via voice commands.
